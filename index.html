Parking

Parking

There is limited parking at the church and on the surrounding residential roads. We suggest that if you are planning to stay for the day, you park your car in Tower Street Car Park (SO23 8TA) or use the city Park & Ride. For other options check http://www.winchester.gov.uk/parking/council-car-parks

Public transport

Winchester Train Station is only a 10 minute walk from the church and reception venue.

Places to eat

  • Union Jacks - pizza by Jamie Oliver
  • The Wykeham Arms - posh pub grub
  • Bishop on the Bridge - good value pub grub

There are lots of other places to eat at the top of town on Jewry street and also up and down the city high street.

Places to stay

  • The West Gate Inn - B&B, VERY close to the venue
  • Winchester Royal Hotel - Forestdale hotel, in the city centre
  • Wessex Hotel - by the Cathedral, city centre
  • Holiday Inn - out of city centre, 10 minute drive

There are also a number of B&Bs to be found on Google.
